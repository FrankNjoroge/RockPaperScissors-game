Rock Paper Scissors, let's brawl!

About

Rock Paper Scissors is a two player game. Each player chooses one of rock, paper or scissors, without knowing the other player's choice. The winner is decided by a set of rules:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Scissors beat paper
  • Paper beats rock

If both players choose the same, there is no winner for that round. For this game, the computer will be one of the players.You get 5 trials to finish the game. To play again, click the play again button.
